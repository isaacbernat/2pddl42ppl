2-players on a single Thumby! Use familiar Pong mechanics against each other in Versus mode. Choose Coop mode to achieve your best time and bouncing stats or even solo mode for a greater individual challenge!

Paddle size, game physics... up to 7 parameters may be adjusted ingame to make the experience unique. All this under 0x100 (256) lines of code. Check it out here or in Github and get inspired to make your own games ;D
